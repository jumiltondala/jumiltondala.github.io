Monexa

Positioning

Monexa is a structured P2P currency exchange built for clarity, operational discipline, and real-world constraints.

The Problem

Informal cross-border exchange systems lack structure, traceability, and proper risk control.

How It Works

Monexa operates with intermediary responsibility, reviewing and tracking each transaction manually.

Business Model

Revenue is generated through controlled spread margins, prioritizing consistency over speculative growth.

Current Stage

The project is operating manually with structured controls, validating logic and risk before automation.
