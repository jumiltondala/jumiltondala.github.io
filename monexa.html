Monexa

What Monexa Is

Monexa is a structured P2P currency exchange operating between Angola and South Africa.

An operational system built for controlled intermediation.

The Problem

Informal exchanges scale poorly, while formal systems introduce cost and rigidity.

Operational Principles

Rules, verification, pricing, and settlement are strictly controlled.

Why This Has Value

Predictability and clarity for people who understand cross-border complexity.
