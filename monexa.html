Monexa

Context

Cross-border currency exchange between Angola and South Africa is often informal, inconsistent and hard to control.

Target Users

Individuals and small operators who already understand the problem and value predictability over convenience.

How It Works

Monexa operates through controlled intermediation, predefined pricing rules and internal verification.

Current Stage

Operational prototype managed through structured spreadsheets and manual verification.

Why It Matters

Monexa reduces uncertainty in environments where formal systems fail and informal ones scale poorly.

© 2026 — Monexa is an operational system, not a promise.
