Monexa

Context

Cross-border currency exchange between Angola and South Africa is often informal, inconsistent and hard to control.

Target Users

Individuals and small operators who value predictability over convenience.

How It Works

Controlled intermediation with predefined rules and verification.

Current Stage

Operational prototype with structured tracking and manual execution.

Why It Matters

Reduces uncertainty where formal systems fail and informal ones scale poorly.
