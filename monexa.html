Monexa

What Monexa Is

Monexa is a structured P2P currency exchange operating between Angola and South Africa.

It is not a marketplace and not a banking alternative. It is an operational system designed to reduce friction in cross-border exchanges through controlled intermediation.

The Type of Problem

The problem Monexa addresses is not access to technology, but access to reliable exchange under real-world constraints.

Informal exchanges scale poorly, while formal systems introduce cost, delay, and rigidity. Monexa operates in the space between these two.
